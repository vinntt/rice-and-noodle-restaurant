OUR STORY

Welcome to Rice & Noodle! Our Chef remembers growing up in his parents’ kitchen, savoring the intensely flavorful, homemade Vietnamese dishes using the fresh ingredients and a variety of spices. He decided to recreate his rich food heritage by opening up his own restaurant in Berlin, his second hometown country. His vision is to bring Vietnamese cuisine to the world and to make it a delicious, pleasant, and memorable experience. Rice & Noodle takes pride in providing customers with a unique cultural food experience, serving Vietnamese Southern-style dishes using fresh ingredients.

OUR MISSION

At Rice & Noodle, we take pride in delivering warm, friendly service and creating mouth-watering culinary delights for all. Using only the freshest locally sourced ingredients, we will ensure you have a dining experience to remember. Rice & Noodle is the perfect place for a romantic meal for two, a catch-up with friends, family parties, business meetings, and bringing loved ones together. With comfortable surroundings, affordable prices, and seating for up to 50 guests, we can cater for all occasions.
